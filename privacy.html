Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026. This policy describes the TABANJI frontend demo.

Information stored

TABANJI may store demo account details, cart contents, favorites, comparisons, settings, reviews and local orders in your browser.

Local storage

Demo information is stored using browser localStorage. It remains on the device and origin where it was created until you remove it or clear browser data.

How information is used

Stored information supports local shopping features, preferences, counters and order demonstrations. This project has no production backend.

Your choices

You can remove demo data through relevant controls or by clearing site data in your browser settings.

Contact

Questions about this demo policy can be sent through the contact page.
