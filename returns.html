Straightforward support

Returns and refunds

Eligible products can be returned within 14 days in this demo policy.

Return conditions

  • Request the return within 14 days.
  • Keep the product and accessories complete.
  • Include proof of purchase.
  • Remove personal information from devices.

Refunds

After inspection, an approved demo refund is returned to the original payment method. Processing may take 3–10 business days.

How to return an item

Start a request

Contact support with your order number.

Prepare the item

Pack the product and included accessories securely.

Send or deliver

Use the provided demo return instructions.

Non-returnable items

Opened hygiene products, personalized goods, digital products and items damaged through misuse may be ineligible, subject to applicable consumer law.
